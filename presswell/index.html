    About estimates

    Phones cannot medically measure blood pressure with a fingerprint. PressWell’s finger check is a wellness estimate. For care decisions, use a validated cuff and talk to a clinician.

    Reading looks very high

    If you feel chest pain, shortness of breath, weakness, or confusion, seek emergency care now. Otherwise rest, recheck with a cuff, and contact a clinician promptly.
