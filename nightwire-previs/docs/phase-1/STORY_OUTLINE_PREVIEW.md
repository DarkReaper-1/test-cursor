# NIGHTWIRE — Story Outline Preview (Phase 1 Seed)

**Full story bible = Phase 3.** This seed locks tone and spine only.

---

## Logline
Detective Mara Quinn is reinstated to close a missing-person file. The motel blood isn’t the victim’s, a ghost radio channel ties the docks to Precinct 7, and the captain wants silence. Quinn can prove the truth — or survive it.

---

## Protagonist Want / Need
- **Want:** Clear the case and keep her badge.  
- **Need:** Decide whether truth is worth burning the institution that employs her.

---

## Spine (Acts)

### Act I — Static
Motel 214 cold scene. First exhibits. Lena Voss pushes a zoning complaint angle. Boone assigns a “clean” narrative (runaway). Board tutorial. Quinn finds the wire residue that shouldn’t exist.

### Act II — Crosstalk
Docks and train-yard wire rooms. Vince Calder as charismatic false gravity. Surveillance and tails. Lab contradictions. A precinct memo with the wrong timestamp. Optional side cases (pager ghosts) feed the main temperature.

### Act III — Dead Air
Identity of the wire’s owner forks. Moral choke: burn a source, protect Lena, or bury the channel. Endings diverge on proof strength + loyalty flags + whether Quinn goes public.

---

## Twist Intent (non-spoiler design)
- At least one major false culprit supported by real exhibits.  
- Institutional antagonist is a system, not only a mustache villain.  
- Victim’s agency reframed mid-campaign (not passive plot device).  

Exact twist map = Phase 3.

---

## Endings (categories)
1. **Print** — Enough proof; costly victory.  
2. **Protection** — Someone walks; Quinn chooses people over record.  
3. **Dead air** — Channel stays dark; world continues raining.
