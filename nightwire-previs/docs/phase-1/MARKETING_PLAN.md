# NIGHTWIRE — Marketing Plan (Phase 1)

---

## 1. Positioning

**One-liner:** A cinematic neo-noir detective game where every clue matters and the city keeps its own schedule.

**Audience:** Fans of narrative investigation games, neo-noir film, crime podcasts, and cinematic single-player experiences on PC + mobile.

**Differentiator:** Film-first atmosphere + living schedule city + board-driven deduction (not QTEs-as-story).

---

## 2. Message Pillars

1. “Feel like the detective in the movie.”  
2. “Port Meridian never stops lying.”  
3. “Your board. Your links. Your ending.”  

---

## 3. Launch Funnel

| Stage | Asset | When |
|-------|-------|------|
| Awareness | Concept trailer (exists) | Now / refresh later |
| Interest | World & cast reveals (Phase 2–3) | After those phases |
| Desire | Gameplay debut (vertical slice) | Post Phase 4 |
| Action | Demo / pre-order / wishlist | Beta window |
| Retain | Post-launch cold case DLC plan (optional) | Post 1.0 |

---

## 4. Channels

- Steam / console-adjacent PC stores · mobile stores  
- YouTube/TikTok short film cuts (rain neon, board ASMR, interrogation)  
- Influencer kits: press build + 5 screenshot moments + logo pack  
- Film-festival-style “interactive noir” press angle  

---

## 5. Key Art

Key art: Quinn in rain, motel neon, board reflected in wet street.  
Logo: NIGHTWIRE wordmark from previs.  
Age rating icons as required per territory.

---

## 6. Community

Dev diaries per phase gate. Avoid spoiling twist matrix. Share systems education (how board links work).

---

## 7. Metrics

Wishlist velocity · trailer CTR · demo completion · sentiment (“cinematic”, “smart”) · mobile crash-free sessions.
