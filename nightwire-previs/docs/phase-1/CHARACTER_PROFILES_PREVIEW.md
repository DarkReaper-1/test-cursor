## Det. Mara Quinn — Protagonist
- **Role:** Reinstated homicide detective  
- **Surface:** Dry, precise, allergic to precinct theater  
- **Pressure fracture:** Was burned on a prior wiretap case; trusts evidence more than people  
- **Gameplay expression:** Vision mastery, board-first thinking, optional empathy or pressure in talks  

## Capt. Elias Boone
- **Role:** Precinct 7 captain  
- **Surface:** Protective mentor optics  
- **Secret tension:** Political survival tied to keeping Nightwire dark  
- **Gameplay:** Quest giver who becomes obstacle; dialogue locked by case temperature  

## Lena Voss
- **Role:** Missing planner’s sister; unreliable ally  
- **Surface:** Grieving, sharp, possibly lying about timelines  
- **Need:** Someone who won’t trade her sister for a clean file  
- **Gameplay:** Major interview tree; can leave city on hard fails  

## Vince Calder
- **Role:** Dock syndicate fixer  
- **Surface:** Charm, alibis, neon booths  
- **Function:** False-lead magnet with real crimes  
- **Gameplay:** Tail sequences, bribe/threat branches, optional non-lethal confrontations  

## Dr. Naomi Park
- **Role:** Forensics  
- **Surface:** Clinical humor, moral compass  
- **Gameplay:** Lab reports, optional ethical warnings about planting/pressuring
