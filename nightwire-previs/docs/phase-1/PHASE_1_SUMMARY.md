## Design locks from Phase 1

- Pillars: cinema first · clues that bite · scheduled city · pressure over power fantasy · moral residue  
- Core loop: investigate → kit/board → confront → fallout  
- Engine proposal: Unreal Engine 5 with scalability tiers (Unity fallback checkpoint)  
- Combat posture: rare, narrative-gated — not shooter-first  
- Platforms: PC + mobile; KBM / gamepad / touch
