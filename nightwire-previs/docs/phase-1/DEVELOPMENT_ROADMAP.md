# NIGHTWIRE — Development Roadmap (Phase 1)

Phased production with **hard approval gates**. No phase starts without sign-off.

---

## Phase Overview

| Phase | Name | Primary outputs | Gate |
|-------|------|-----------------|------|
| 0 | Previs (done) | Concept trailer, vision deck | Approved |
| **1** | **GDD (this pack)** | Design bible + companions | **Awaiting** |
| 2 | World Design | District dossiers, map, POIs, traversal | Wait |
| 3 | Story & Characters | Full outline, profiles, twist/ending matrix | Wait |
| 4 | Core Gameplay Systems | Vertical slice systems playable | Wait |
| 5 | UI/UX | Full HUD/board/kit flows + mobile | Wait |
| 6 | AI Systems | Schedules, memory, perception | Wait |
| 7 | Audio & Music | Adaptive score, beds, VO pass plan | Wait |
| 8 | Optimization | Tiers, profiling, device lab | Wait |
| 9 | Testing & Bug Fixing | QA plans, regression, softlock hunts | Wait |
| 10 | Final Polish | Trailer final, store page, certification | Wait |

---

## Milestone Map (post-approval)

1. **Vertical Slice** — Chapter 1 playable (systems proof)  
2. **Alpha** — Acts I–II content graybox + systems complete  
3. **Beta** — Full campaign, temp art OK, content lock approaching  
4. **RC** — Polish, certification, store assets  
5. **Launch** — See `LAUNCH_CHECKLIST.md`  

Exact calendar dates intentionally omitted; milestones are gated by quality and approvals, not fixed week counts.

---

## Parallel Tracks (after Phase 4 starts)

- Narrative ↔ Systems ↔ Environment art ↔ Character art ↔ Audio  
- Weekly playtests once slice exists  
- Device lab continuous from first mobile cook  

---

## Dependencies

- Phase 2 before heavy environment production  
- Phase 3 before VO cast & major mocap  
- Phase 4 slice before marketing “gameplay debut” trailer  
- Phase 8 before store performance claims  

---

## Team Shape (indicative)

Design · Narrative · Eng (gameplay/engine/UI) · Env art · Char art · Animation · Audio · QA · Prod · Tech art  

Staffing scales with phase; Phase 1 is documentation-complete with no full engine staffing required yet.
