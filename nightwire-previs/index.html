NIGHTWIRE

Announcement Pitch

NIGHTWIRE is a cinematic 3D detective game blending 1980s–1990s neo-noir crime films with modern investigation gameplay. You play Detective Mara Quinn in Port Meridian — a rainy, neon-soaked semi-open city where every district hides environmental stories, schedules, and lies.

This package is a concept trailer + previs only: camera language, sample systems UI, atmosphere, and logo. Full documentation and production start only after your greenlight.

  • Tone: dark, rainy, suspenseful, cinematic, immersive
  • Fantasy: feel like the lead in a premium crime thriller
  • Platforms (planned): Windows, macOS, Linux optional, Android, iOS
  • Controls (planned): KBM, controller, touch

Story Hook (Trailer Cut)

A city planner vanishes from the Meridian Motel. The blood on the sill isn’t hers. A ghost radio channel links the docks to Precinct 7. Captain Boone wants the file closed. Quinn was reinstated to rubber-stamp it — she refuses.

Full multi-chapter bible, twists, false suspects, moral branches, and endings are Phase 1–3 deliverables after approval. Trailer promise: every clue matters; the wire never lies — people do.
